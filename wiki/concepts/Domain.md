# Domain

Per [[EricEvans]]: *"A sphere of knowledge, influence, or activity. The subject area to which the user applies a program is the domain of the software."*

The domain is the *real-world* problem space — insurance underwriting, logistics, payroll, audio production. The [[DomainModel]] is a software representation of selected aspects of the domain. Inside the domain, the [[CoreDomain]] is the slice that most differentiates the business and most rewards careful modeling.

## Connections
- [[DomainModel]] — software abstraction of the domain.
- [[CoreDomain]] — the highest-value slice.
