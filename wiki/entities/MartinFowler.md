# Martin Fowler

Software-engineering author and Chief Scientist at ThoughtWorks. Best known for *Refactoring*, *Patterns of Enterprise Application Architecture* (P of EAA), and the long-running [bliki](https://martinfowler.com/bliki/) of short pattern-style essays.

## Known Work
- *Patterns of Enterprise Application Architecture* — defines [[DomainModel]], Service Layer, Transaction Script, Layer Supertype, etc.
- bliki — short essays including [[anemic-domain-model]] and POJO.
- Cited by [[EricEvans]] in [[ddd-reference-2015]] for "definitive documentation of emerging patterns" in DDD.

## Recurring Themes
- Object-orientation in earnest — combining data and process, against procedural-style "service over data" designs.
- Pragmatic, vocabulary-establishing prose: he names patterns so practitioners can argue about them.

## Connections
- [[EricEvans]] — collaborator on DDD pattern communication.
- [[AnemicDomainModel]] — coined and named by Fowler.
- [[DomainDrivenDesign]] — major popularizer.
