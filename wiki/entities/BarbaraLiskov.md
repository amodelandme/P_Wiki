# Barbara Liskov

MIT computer scientist, Turing Award laureate (2008), and originator of the [[LiskovSubstitutionPrinciple]] — formalized in her 1987 keynote and 1994 paper with Jeannette Wing on subtypes.

## Connections
- [[LiskovSubstitutionPrinciple]] — namesake principle, the "L" in SOLID.
- [[RobertCMartin]] — popularized LSP via *Clean Architecture* and the SOLID acronym.
