# Robert C. Martin

"Uncle Bob" — author of *Clean Code*, *Clean Architecture*, and *Agile Software Development: Principles, Patterns, and Practices*. Coined the **SOLID** acronym for object-oriented design principles, including L = [[LiskovSubstitutionPrinciple]].

## Known Work
- *Clean Architecture* — the book that prompted the [[liskov-italicized-paragraph-explained]] conversation.
- SOLID principles formulation.
- Cited in [[introduction-to-domain-driven-design]] for SRP and SOLID resources.

## Connections
- [[LiskovSubstitutionPrinciple]] — popularizer.
- [[SingleResponsibilityPrinciple]] — coined the modern SRP framing.
- [[BarbaraLiskov]] — formalized the principle Martin popularized.
