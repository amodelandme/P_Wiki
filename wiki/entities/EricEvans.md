# Eric Evans

Founder of Domain Language, Inc. and author of *Domain-Driven Design: Tackling Complexity in the Heart of Software* (2004) — the "Big Blue Book" that named and codified [[DomainDrivenDesign]]. In 2015 he published the CC-BY-licensed [[ddd-reference-2015]] as a free pattern-summary companion.

## Known Work
- *Domain-Driven Design: Tackling Complexity in the Heart of Software* (2004).
- *Domain-Driven Design Reference: Definitions and Pattern Summaries* (2015).
- Originator of the term [[UbiquitousLanguage]]; canonical formulations of [[BoundedContext]], [[Entity]], [[ValueObject]], [[Aggregate]], [[Repository]], [[DomainService]].
- Added [[DomainEvent]] and Partnership patterns post-2004.

## Connections
- [[DomainDrivenDesign]] — formalized the discipline.
- [[MartinFowler]] — frequent collaborator on pattern documentation.
- [[AnemicDomainModel]] — co-observer with Fowler of the anti-pattern.
